For too long, corporations have abused their power to exploit people and influence our elected officials to implement harmful policy. When those in power do not serve the interests of the people, the people can effect change by wielding the profound power that they all have: their labour power. Together, the people are strong.

Unions and Striking workers have a long history of being catalysts for policy which serves the interests of the people and gives the striking workers a more fair share of the value they create. This website's purpose is to amplify the labor movement and streamline the process of standing in solidarity with striking workers and buildling labor movement networks.
